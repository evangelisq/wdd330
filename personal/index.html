Welcome to Vancouver Paradise

CONNECTING, SUPPORTING, AND ADVOCATING FOR VANCOUVER PARADISE.

Vancouver is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse attractions. Highlights include Stanley Park with its scenic Seawall and towering trees, the thrilling Capilano Suspension Bridge, and the nearby Grouse Mountain for outdoor adventures year-round. Cultural gems like the Museum of Anthropology and historic Gastown offer rich history and local charm, while the Vancouver Aquarium and VanDusen Botanical Garden provide family-friendly fun and peaceful nature escapes-all within minutes of the city center.
